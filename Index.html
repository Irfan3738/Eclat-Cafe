About Us

Welcome to Eclat Cafe, your sweet escape in the heart of Bangalore.
Our passion is crafting the perfect milkshake, and we've turned it into an art.

We believe that every sip should be a moment of pure joy. That's why we use
only the finest ingredients to create our wide range of milkshakes, from timeless
classics to unique, new flavors.

Beyond our delicious drinks, Eclat Cafe is a place to relax and connect.
We've created a cozy space where you can unwind, chat with friends,
and make new memories.

Come visit us and find your favorite treat.
We can't wait to share our passion with you.
